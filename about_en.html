DS - GameStudio is an independent game development studio from Moldova, founded in 2023 with one goal: to create unique, atmospheric, and memorable game worlds.

🚀 Our Beginning It all started in early 2023, when a group of friends, united by a love for video games, decided to turn their passion into a profession. We are a team from Moldova — a country rich in culture, which inspires us to create original stories and visual styles. Our first steps were simple projects — 2D platformers and mini-games. But month by month, we improved, explored game engines in depth, experimented with genres and mechanics, and learned to work like a true studio.

🎮 Who We Are Today,

DS - GameStudio is:

👨‍💻 A united team of developers, game designers, artists, and writers;

🌍 Projects that resonate not only in Moldova but beyond its borders;

🛠 Constant growth and adoption of new technologies (Unity, Godot, Unreal Engine, and more);

❤️ A sincere love for games and for the people who play them.

✨ Our Games

Among our flagship projects:

Thronebound — a fantasy 2D platformer about a knight in a forgotten kingdom; TBS - TotalBattleStrike — a fast-paced modern military shooter.

Each game we create is a world where gameplay blends with story — every level crafted with care and passion.

🔮 What’s Next?

We’re not planning to stop. Our ambition is to reach the international market, take part in game expos, launch large-scale projects — and perhaps one day, build our own game universe.

We believe that big ideas are born in small studios. And it’s those ideas that drive us forward.
